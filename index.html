BACP Workshop: Bridging AI and Clinical Practice

Date 20 March 2026 Time 08:30–18:30 Venue KCL Waterloo, FWB G.73 Capacity Up to 100 attendees

About

A one-day in-person workshop bringing together AI engineers and healthcare professionals. The day will include invited talks and round-table discussions to identify barriers to adoption, share best practice, and seed future translational collaborations.

Format

Invited talks • Round-table discussions • Networking

Outputs

  • White paper statement on barriers to adoption and implementation of AI in practice
  • Concepts for future funded research projects to enhance translational AI work
